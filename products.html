RadioKit Limited products:

it's like a google docs for radios

RadioKit focuses mainly on delivering software services for various type of broadcasters - from small independent initiatives, through networks of Dj's collectives to internet and FM / DAB+ radio stations.

It is quite obvious that in that range of customers we're fullfing very different needs. Due to that, from the very beginning we were thinking about our products as Lego blocks - they by design work with each other creating a complete solution for radio.

Pick the one you need - have a try and you'll see you'll love it.

Electron / Joint

Broadcasting Van in your pocket

Prices of hardware codecs doesn't seem right in times of powerful computers in our pockets and high speed data transfer.

This is why we wanted to solve Audio over IP problem just with proper software. This however was not an easy fight. After two years of struggle and we hit the dead end - none of the existing technologies was capable of doing that on a satisfactory level for 24/7 broadcasting environment.

So...

We created our own framework in which we created Joint & Electron. First is a simple point-to-point solution, and second allows you to control all of your "joints" in a webbrowser, mixing sound between them. And all of that in SaaS model, so you don't need any additional infrastructure. Just your existing computers, phones, tablets, or ... raspberry Pi!

Freezer

Streams recording in cloud

Some needs that by law, some for monitoring, some for re-broadcasts. Whatever is your need - freezer should come handy for you.

You can simply tell us what is your stream http address and from that moment we can give you an access to a searchable repository with 24/7 recordings of your stream, divided in chunks of your preference.

There is of course more to it and the solution can be tailored to your specific needs, to gather metadata you would like, notify you if it detects something or extract stats of what's there.
